How to save your LinkedIn profile as a PDF:
  1. Open your profile on your computer (not mobile)
  2. Look for the row of buttons just below your Follower and Connection numbers. You will see buttons including Open To and a circle with three dots (•••)
  3. Click the circle with three dots
  4. Select Save to PDF
  5. Your browser will download a PDF file. Upload that file below.
